About Me

I'm Otabil Ishaque, a Data Analyst and Machine Learning Enthusiast from Ghana. I work with data to turn complex information into actionable insights. I enjoy transforming raw data into strategic decisions through data analysis, machine learning, and visualization. My role involves building and refining data models, creating visualizations, and deriving actionable insights to drive business decisions. I add a personal touch to every project, ensuring that each analysis and visualization effectively communicates the message and identity of the business. My goal is to harness the power of data to unlock valuable insights and support strategic decision-making. I've applied my skills to a variety of real-world projects and industries.
